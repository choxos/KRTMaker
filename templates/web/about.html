About KRT Maker

Automated Key Resource Table generation for bioRxiv preprints

Our Mission

Making research reproducibility easier by automating the creation of Key Resource Tables (KRTs) that comply with ASAP Open Science guidelines.

We believe that transparent research resource reporting shouldn't be a burden. Our platform leverages advanced AI and pattern recognition to automatically extract key resources from bioRxiv preprints, generating standardized tables that enhance reproducibility and scientific transparency.

Pattern Recognition
